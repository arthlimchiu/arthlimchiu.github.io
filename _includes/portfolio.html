RipeMango





Are you the person who forgets the movies you should've watched?


RipeMango is a simple app to discover movies and add movies you like to your watchlist.


  • Discover popular, upcoming and top rated movies
  • Bookmark movies to add them to your watchlist
  • View your watchlist and remove movies when you have watched them already




Made with: Kotlin, RxJava2, Dagger2, Retrofit2, Glide, Android Architecture Components
